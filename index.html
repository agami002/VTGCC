Why Qatar, Why Now

5-Year Rollout Plan – Qatar

Delivery model

In Qatar, we propose a disciplined rollout targeting 25 factories per year over the first 5 years, a total of 125 factories. Each factory engagement runs on an average timeline of 3.5 months per project, with multiple factories running in parallel.

  • Target: 25 new factories per year × 5 years = 125 factories.
  • Timeline: ≈3.5 months per factory project (from discovery to go-live).
  • Parallel delivery: several factories in-flight at any time to optimise resource utilisation.

Resource ratios (rule of thumb):

  • 1 Adoption Manager per 8 factories (all-inclusive: 20k QAR/month).
  • 1 Solution Lead per 6 factories (all-inclusive: 18k QAR/month).
  • 2 App Builders per 5 factories (all-inclusive: 15k QAR/month each).

At 25 factories per year these ratios imply, at steady state, approximately 3–4 Adoption Managers, 4–5 Solution Leads, and ~10 App Builders serving the Qatar portfolio.
